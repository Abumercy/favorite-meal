The Recipe of My Favorite Meal

The name of my favorite meal is Cinnamon Roll
It is energy-giving food made with wheat flour

The Steps/Methods of preparation

  1. Put the flour on a bowl
  2. Add yeast to it
  3. Add sugar to it
  4. Add salt to it
  5. Add the milk
  6. Add Milk flavour
  7. Add the Margarine
  8. Add the egg to it
  9. Then mixed them thoroughly together until it become very soft
  10. Flatten the dough
  11. Add the brown sugar and the Cinnamon powder together in a separate bowl and stir
  12. Then sprinkle the mixture of brown sugar and cinnamon powder on the surface of the flatten dough
  13. Roll it accordingly, attach your thread, fold to cut to your desired sizes
  14. Allow the roll to stay for 20 minutes
  15. Then set it on baking pan for baking
  16. Bake until it becomes browish
  17. Finally serve yourself
